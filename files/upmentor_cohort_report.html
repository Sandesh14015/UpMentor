Credentials Issued

What students earned

The UpMentor 4-credential stack is designed so that each credential requires more from the student than the last. Not everyone earns all four.

116
Course Completion Certificates
Issued to all students who completed the 6-module program. Verified, unique ID, publicly checkable at verify.upmentor.in
18
Internship Certificates
Awarded after completing a real project with an UpMentor employer partner. 15.5% of all enrolled students.
31
Outstanding Performance Awards
Top 25% of completers by composite score. Selective enough to carry meaning. 26.7% of completers.
14
Letters of Recommendation issued
LORs were issued to students who scored in the top 12% AND completed an internship. Each LOR is personalised — written from the student's specific project, scores, and observed work ethic. Not a template.
