1. Purpose of this Agreement

This Agreement establishes a mutual understanding between UpMentor Edutech Private Limited ("UpMentor") and the Employer Partner named above. The purpose is to formalise credential recognition, provide the Employer Partner with early access to UpMentor-certified talent, and enable both parties to build a trusted pipeline between education and employment.

This is not a financial contract. Neither party is obligated to make any payment as a result of signing this Agreement. No hiring quota is imposed.

4. Confidentiality & Data

Student profile data shared under this Agreement is for recruitment purposes only. The Employer Partner agrees not to share student personal data with third parties without the student's explicit consent. UpMentor will not share Employer Partner internal data with competitor organisations.

5. Term & Termination

This Agreement is valid for one calendar year from the date of signing and auto-renews unless either party provides 30 days' written notice of termination. Either party may exit at any time with written notice — no penalty applies.

6. Governing law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of India. Any disputes shall first be resolved through good-faith negotiation. If unresolved, disputes shall be subject to arbitration in Bhubaneswar, Odisha.
